NIFTY SELLER LITE — V2.1 PRE-MARKET INTEGRITY AUDIT

ARCHITECTURE
- Verified exactly one final strategy brain exists in analysis/decision.py.
- Verified SnapshotService calls the final brain exactly once.
- The compact evidence table remains display-only and cannot feed the decision.
- Planner, Execution Guard and Position Guardian remain downstream read-only modules.

FRESHNESS AND FEED INTEGRITY
- LIVE session now requires both a fresh NIFTY quote and a fresh completed
  current-session 1-minute candle.
- Adds separate live-age handling for India VIX and Top-7 quotes.
- Stale live NIFTY-futures volume is neutralized inside core evidence.
- NIFTY last price must be positive and finite.
- Option-chain integrity requires CE and PE rows, a complete ATM CE/PE pair,
  enough positive premiums and bounded option-spot/NIFTY divergence.
- Invalid option structure blocks option intelligence, strike planning and execution.

OPTIONS CALCULATION
- Intraday option comparison only uses a recent prior snapshot.
- A continuity gap resets to DAY CHANGE — CONTINUITY RESET / WARMING UP.
- Persistence cannot bridge a stale snapshot gap.
- PCR is context only; it cannot create standalone bullish/bearish direction or
  double-count OI already represented by premium+OI+volume flow.

DECISION AND DISPLAY
- CE, PE and Iron Condor receive only strategy-relevant level cautions.
- Compact evidence rounding uses a largest-remainder method so displayed
  directional rows total 100.0 at one-decimal precision.
- Position Guardian freezes the planner's exact entry-credit estimate.
- Instrument master cache refreshes after 24 hours and safely falls back to an
  existing cache during a temporary download failure.

No auto order placement, broker modification or automatic exit was added.
