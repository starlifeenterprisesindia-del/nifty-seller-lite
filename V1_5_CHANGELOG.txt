NIFTY SELLER LITE — V1.5 EXECUTION GUARD

MAJOR ADDITIONS
- Added a post-decision Execution Guard without adding a second strategy brain.
- Added two-consecutive-fresh-snapshot confirmation before ENTRY READY.
- Added configurable entry start, no-new-entry time and compulsory exit time.
- Added editable capital, risk percentage, current lot size and maximum-lot cap.
- Added protected-plan risk per lot and rupee risk-budget sizing.
- Added estimated target-credit capture, target exit debit, premium-loss trigger,
  stop exit debit and rupee estimates.
- Added spot invalidation guidance from canonical price action or Condor breakevens.
- Added manual one-trade/day journal: Trade Taken, Target/Manual Exit and SL Hit.
- Marking a trade locks the local day against another entry.
- Added same-day bounded signal history and discipline persistence using atomic writes.
- Added Feed Integrity visibility for discipline state.

SAFETY / ARCHITECTURE
- The only final strategy brain remains analysis/decision.py.
- The strike planner remains post-decision and cannot change strategy scores.
- The execution guard remains post-plan and cannot change the selected setup.
- No broker-order, auto-entry, auto-exit or credential storage code was added.
- Reference-only market sessions can never show ENTRY READY.
- WAIT from the final brain cannot be overridden by the execution guard.
- One trade/day is the default hard discipline rule.
- Current NIFTY lot size is editable rather than silently assumed forever.
- Cleaned a duplicate LevelBundle annotation from models.py.

DEFAULT RISK PROFILE
- Capital: ₹2,50,000
- Maximum risk: 0.5%
- Lot cap: 1
- Entry window: 10:15 to 11:30
- Target credit capture: 35%
- Spread-loss trigger: 40% of collected credit
- Compulsory exit: 14:30

All risk inputs remain editable in the sidebar. Calculations are read-only estimates.
