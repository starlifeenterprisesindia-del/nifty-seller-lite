V0.5 CORE MARKET ENGINE — CHANGELOG

ADDED
- Canonical technical utility module for completed candles, ATR and confirmed swings.
- 3m/15m Price Action structure, event, move stage, invalidation and evidence scores.
- Confluence-based Support/Resistance zones with source strength and remaining room.
